At the time, these were some of the features and small annoyances I hoped Apple would improve in iOS 7.

## Videos
- Bring back playlists.
- Allow video playback when the app is minimized.
- Allow other video apps to play files with DRM support.
- Allow editing of TV episode metadata, such as the date added.

## Built-in apps
- Allow built-in apps like Stocks and Weather to be turned off or hidden.
- Remove the separate Contacts app, since contacts are already part of the Phone app.
- Move the Clock app to the time display when tapping the time on screen.

## Camera Roll
- Bulk delete pictures. Enough said.

## Books
- In iTunes sync, add a button to uncheck or recheck all checkboxes.
